# Reviewer Attacks

## Attack: The v1 simulator makes retries free.

Sustained. V2 adds retry cost and persistent faults.

## Attack: More containment can hide stale unsafe state.

Sustained. At persistent-fault probability 0.25, eight retries reduce propagation to 0.038 but raise masked unsafe rate to 0.074 and lower utility to 0.841.

## Attack: This is only a toy simulator.

Sustained. Decision: workshop-only.

## Attack: The paper should not claim universal superiority over runtime assurance or behavior trees.

Sustained. The claim is narrowed to a containment-budget reporting and architecture mechanism.
